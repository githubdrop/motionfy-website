# BioLumina - Life Sciences Marketing Agency

## Original Problem Statement
Build a Life Sciences and Biotech marketing agency website that looks legitimate and professional.

## Architecture
- **Frontend**: React with Framer Motion animations, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI components
- **Backend**: FastAPI with MongoDB
- **Database**: MongoDB for contact submissions and newsletter subscriptions

## User Personas
1. **Biotech Startup Founders** - Looking for marketing expertise to launch products
2. **Pharma Marketing Directors** - Need agency support for campaigns
3. **Medical Device Companies** - Seeking regulatory-compliant marketing
4. **Investors** - Evaluating company credibility

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Professional multi-page marketing website
- Contact form with lead capture
- Case studies showcase
- Team/about section
- Blog/resources section
- Services overview
